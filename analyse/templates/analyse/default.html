What is Twitter Opinions?

Twitter opinions is a simple program which allows you to enter a topic of your choice and then find out what other people think! Created with Python 3.6.2, you can gather hundreds of tweets in only a few seconds and see if they have an overall positive, mixed or negative opinion on whatever you want! Try it out now!
